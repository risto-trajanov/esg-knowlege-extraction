 </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>Chevron Corporation </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>2018 Annual Meeting of Stockholders </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>Additional Information Relating to a Stockholder Proposal </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; margin-left:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>Item&nbsp;7 on the proxy card: Stockholder proposal regarding report on methane emissions:</B> </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; margin-left:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Resolved:<B> </B>Shareholders request that Chevron provide a report (at reasonable cost, omitting proprietary information) using quantitative
indicators, on the company&#146;s actions beyond regulatory requirements to minimize methane emissions, particularly leakage, from the company&#146;s hydraulic fracturing operations. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The Board of Directors of Chevron Corporation has recommended that stockholders vote against Item 7 for the reasons set forth on page 73 of the Proxy
Statement (the &#147;2018 Proxy Statement&#148;) for the 2018 annual meeting of stockholders (https://www.chevron.com/-/media/shared-media/documents/chevron-proxy-statement-2018.pdf). </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Chevron would like to update stockholders on our activities relating to methane. To actively and constructively contribute to the dialogue about methane
management with our peers and stakeholders, effective May&nbsp;10, 2018, Chevron has become a signatory to the <I>Oil and Gas Methane Partnership Guiding Principles</I> (the &#147;Guiding Principles&#148;). The Guiding Principles, described below,
establish priority areas for action for managing methane emissions along the natural gas value chain. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The Guiding Principles were established
collaboratively with industry, international institutions, <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">non-government</FONT> organizations, and academics to achieve the following: </P> <P STYLE="font-size:6pt;margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt">&nbsp;</P>
<TABLE STYLE="BORDER-COLLAPSE:COLLAPSE; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:10pt" BORDER="0" CELLPADDING="0" CELLSPACING="0" WIDTH="100%">
<TR style = "page-break-inside:avoid">
<TD WIDTH="4%" VALIGN="top" ALIGN="left">1.</TD>
<TD ALIGN="left" VALIGN="top">Continually reduce methane emissions: establish plans to systematically monitor and reduce methane emissions from operated assets, prioritizing higher emitting assets. </TD></TR></TABLE>
<P STYLE="font-size:6pt;margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt">&nbsp;</P>
<TABLE STYLE="BORDER-COLLAPSE:COLLAPSE; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:10pt" BORDER="0" CELLPADDING="0" CELLSPACING="0" WIDTH="100%">
<TR style = "page-break-inside:avoid">
<TD WIDTH="4%" VALIGN="top" ALIGN="left">2.</TD>
<TD ALIGN="left" VALIGN="top">Advance strong performance along the gas value chains: work to help improve approaches to and the application of methane management including estimation, detection, abatement, and reporting practices. </TD></TR></TABLE>
<P STYLE="font-size:6pt;margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt">&nbsp;</P>
<TABLE STYLE="BORDER-COLLAPSE:COLLAPSE; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:10pt" BORDER="0" CELLPADDING="0" CELLSPACING="0" WIDTH="100%">
<TR style = "page-break-inside:avoid">
<TD WIDTH="4%" VALIGN="top" ALIGN="left">3.</TD>
<TD ALIGN="left" VALIGN="top">Improve accuracy of methane emissions data: support research that improves the quantification of methane emissions and make progress towards verifiable reductions. </TD></TR></TABLE>
<P STYLE="font-size:6pt;margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt">&nbsp;</P>
<TABLE STYLE="BORDER-COLLAPSE:COLLAPSE; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:10pt" BORDER="0" CELLPADDING="0" CELLSPACING="0" WIDTH="100%">
<TR style = "page-break-inside:avoid">
<TD WIDTH="4%" VALIGN="top" ALIGN="left">4.</TD>
<TD ALIGN="left" VALIGN="top">Advocate sound policy and regulations on methane emissions: support policies that incentivize early action, drive performance improvements, facilitate proper enforcement, and support flexibility and innovation.
</TD></TR></TABLE> <P STYLE="font-size:6pt;margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt">&nbsp;</P>
<TABLE STYLE="BORDER-COLLAPSE:COLLAPSE; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:10pt" BORDER="0" CELLPADDING="0" CELLSPACING="0" WIDTH="100%">
<TR style = "page-break-inside:avoid">
<TD WIDTH="4%" VALIGN="top" ALIGN="left">5.</TD>
<TD ALIGN="left" VALIGN="top">Increase transparency: provide information in our external reports on methane emissions data, methodologies used to derive the data, the progress and challenges in methane emissions management, and support the
standardization of comparable external methane reporting. </TD></TR></TABLE> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The Guiding Principles are aligned with three best practices that Chevron has
committed to implementing for our U.S. onshore operations through <I>The Environmental Partnership</I>, a U.S.-based coalition among oil and gas companies described on page 73 of the 2018 Proxy Statement: </P>
<P STYLE="font-size:6pt;margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt">&nbsp;</P>
<TABLE STYLE="BORDER-COLLAPSE:COLLAPSE; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:10pt" BORDER="0" CELLPADDING="0" CELLSPACING="0" WIDTH="100%">
<TR style = "page-break-inside:avoid">
<TD WIDTH="4%" VALIGN="top" ALIGN="left">1.</TD>
<TD ALIGN="left" VALIGN="top">Leak detection and repair: timely repair of leaky equipment and implementing initial monitoring at selected sites to detect methane leaks. </TD></TR></TABLE>
<P STYLE="font-size:6pt;margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt">&nbsp;</P>
<TABLE STYLE="BORDER-COLLAPSE:COLLAPSE; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:10pt" BORDER="0" CELLPADDING="0" CELLSPACING="0" WIDTH="100%">
<TR style = "page-break-inside:avoid">
<TD WIDTH="4%" VALIGN="top" ALIGN="left">2.</TD>
<TD ALIGN="left" VALIGN="top">Pneumatic controller: replace, remove, or retrofit high-bleed pneumatic controllers with lower- or <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">zero-bleed</FONT> methane emissions controllers. </TD></TR></TABLE>
<P STYLE="font-size:6pt;margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt">&nbsp;</P>
<TABLE STYLE="BORDER-COLLAPSE:COLLAPSE; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:10pt" BORDER="0" CELLPADDING="0" CELLSPACING="0" WIDTH="100%">
<TR style = "page-break-inside:avoid">
<TD WIDTH="4%" VALIGN="top" ALIGN="left">3.</TD>
<TD ALIGN="left" VALIGN="top">Manual liquids unloading: implement best practices to minimize emissions during removal of liquids. </TD></TR></TABLE> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Signing
on to the Guiding Principles, in addition to being a founding member of The Environmental Partnership, is a recent example of Chevron&#146;s significant and ongoing efforts to reduce methane emissions from its operations and demonstrates its
commitment to protecting the environment. In light of these and other Chevron activities, commitments, and investments, the report requested in the stockholder proposal is unnecessary. Chevron urges you to vote AGAINST Item 7. </P>

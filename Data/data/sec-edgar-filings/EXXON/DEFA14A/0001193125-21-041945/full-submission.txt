<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">As we move forward, our challenge now is to continue to find ways to safely drive further cost efficiencies and generate the most value from all of our

<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">If you haven&#146;t already done so, I encourage you to learn more about our plans by reading the 2021 Energy and Carbon Summary, which is posted on our

<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">That&#146;s part of the thinking behind our newest business &#150; ExxonMobil Low Carbon Solutions &#150; that was established to commercialize and deploy

<Center><DIV STYLE="width:8.5in" align="left">
 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">We&#146;re also continuing to invest in R&amp;D focused on the world&#146;s highest emitting sectors:
manufacturing, commercial transportation, and power generation, which together account for 80&nbsp;percent of global energy-related carbon emissions and where today&#146;s alternatives are insufficient. </P>

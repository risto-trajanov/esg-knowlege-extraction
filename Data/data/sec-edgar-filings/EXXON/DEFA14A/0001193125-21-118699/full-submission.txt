<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">India has rising energy demands and that&#146;s going to lead to rising emissions, but I believe with the right technology we can overcome that challenge </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">And that is only going to be possible through a mix of energy sources to progress India to the next level, and gas is one of the key elements of this mix </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">It&#146;s going to be a mammoth task but that&#146;s where we have the opportunity to play a very important role with gas </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Different industries have different usages of natural gas in India, fertilisers is a big industry </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Natural gas is used as a feedstock for ammonia which is used to produce fertilisers </P>

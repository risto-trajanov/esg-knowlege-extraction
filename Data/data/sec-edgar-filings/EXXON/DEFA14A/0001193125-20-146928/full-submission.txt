business operations and engagement efforts on important issues such as managing climate risk. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; line-height:26.0pt; font-size:14pt; font-family:ARIAL">I want to take this opportunity to address two

 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:14pt; font-family:ARIAL"><B>Managing Climate Risk and Developing Solutions </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:24pt; margin-bottom:0pt; line-height:26.0pt; font-size:14pt; font-family:ARIAL">Society faces a dual challenge of increasing supplies of energy to meet the demand of a growing world population while also addressing the risks of
climate change. Our company plays an important role in addressing both aspects of that challenge. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; line-height:26.0pt; font-size:14pt; font-family:ARIAL">Our <B><I>Energy&nbsp;&amp; Carbon
Summary</I></B>, which is available on our website, is a good resource for shareholders to understand how our company approaches risks related to climate change. The report analyzes potential impacts to the company&#146;s business under 2&deg;C
scenarios in which demand for our products may be influenced by policy, technology, and consumer choices consistent with the Paris Agreement. Also included<I> </I>is information about how we mitigate those potential impacts and ensure our physical
assets, including our petrochemical investments, are able to withstand risks from storms and other events. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; line-height:26.0pt; font-size:14pt; font-family:ARIAL">The
<I></I><B><I>Energy</I></B><I></I><B><I>&nbsp;&amp; Carbon Summary</I></B><I></I> highlights our research and technology program that supports lower-emission solutions in transportation, power generation, and industrial applications &#150; sectors
which today account for more than 80&nbsp;percent of global carbon emissions. The program includes work on advanced biofuels, lower-emission manufacturing and carbon capture, and is carried out at our corporate laboratories, in collaboration with
leading universities and the U.S. National Laboratories, and in partnership with innovative <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">start-ups.</FONT> </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; line-height:26.0pt; font-size:14pt; font-family:ARIAL">The report also highlights how we are working to reduce emissions in our operations through efficiency gains and new technologies. This includes our
efforts to achieve a <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">15-percent</FONT> reduction in methane emissions and a <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">25-percent</FONT> reduction in flaring by the end of this year versus 2016 levels. </P>

 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; line-height:26.0pt; font-size:14pt; font-family:ARIAL">Our risk-management approach recognizes the central role that policy will play in mitigating
climate risks, and the <I></I><B><I>Energy</I></B><I></I><B><I>&nbsp;&amp; Carbon Summary</I></B><I></I> outlines the company&#146;s engagements on climate-related policy, including our support for carbon-pricing and for policies that bolster the

also provide rigorous oversight to key risks associated with our business. In response to feedback received from shareholders on this topic, this year we expanded the <I></I><B><I>Energy&nbsp;&amp; Carbon Summary</I></B><I></I> to include enhanced
disclosure of the Board&#146;s oversight framework and process, including the roles of our Board committees, as they apply to climate-related risk. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:24pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:14pt; font-family:ARIAL"><B>Expanding
Shareholder Engagement </B></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; line-height:26.0pt; font-size:14pt; font-family:ARIAL">We&#146;ve been significantly expanding shareholder engagement for the past five years, and in 2019 we held more
than 85 engagements with investors, pension funds and other organizations on environmental, social and governance issues. This includes more than two dozen engagements with signatories of Climate Action 100+, including with the Church of England and
the New York State Common Retirement Fund. Individuals at all levels of our organization were included in these substantive and productive engagements, including subject-matter experts and independent Directors. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; line-height:26.0pt; font-size:14pt; font-family:ARIAL">In direct response to feedback gained through these engagements and shareholder input, the Board strengthened the authorities of our independent
Lead Director, resulting in a greatly expanded oversight role. This change, as detailed in the proxy, enhances the Board&#146;s already thorough oversight of management and our CEO on issues important to the business, including the risk of climate
change. Other examples of the Board&#146;s responsiveness to shareholders include adding a new provision for shareholders to call special meetings, and enhanced disclosures on issues of importance, including risks related to climate change and
oversight of lobbying and political contributions. </P>

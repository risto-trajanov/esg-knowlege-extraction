<P STYLE="margin-top:24pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>ExxonMobil Expands Renewable Fuels Agreement with Global Clean Energy Holdings </B></P>
<P STYLE="font-size:12pt;margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt">&nbsp;</P>
<TABLE STYLE="BORDER-COLLAPSE:COLLAPSE; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:10pt" BORDER="0" CELLPADDING="0" CELLSPACING="0" WIDTH="100%">
<TR style = "page-break-inside:avoid">

barrels per year </P></TD></TR></TABLE> <P STYLE="font-size:6pt;margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt">&nbsp;</P>

<TD ALIGN="left" VALIGN="top"> <P ALIGN="left" STYLE=" margin-top:0pt ; margin-bottom:0pt; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:10pt">Renewable fuel using Global Clean Energy&#146;s patented camelina crop can significantly reduce greenhouse gas
emissions </P></TD></TR></TABLE> <P STYLE="font-size:6pt;margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt">&nbsp;</P>
<TABLE STYLE="BORDER-COLLAPSE:COLLAPSE; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:10pt" BORDER="0" CELLPADDING="0" CELLSPACING="0" WIDTH="100%">

<TD ALIGN="left" VALIGN="top"> <P ALIGN="left" STYLE=" margin-top:0pt ; margin-bottom:0pt; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:10pt">Biorefinery startup on schedule for early 2022 </P></TD></TR></TABLE>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">IRVING, Texas &#150; ExxonMobil and Global Clean Energy have expanded their five-year agreement to increase ExxonMobil&#146;s purchase of renewable diesel up
to 5&nbsp;million barrels per year. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">ExxonMobil will be the exclusive buyer of renewable diesel from Global Clean Energy&#146;s biorefinery in
Bakersfield, California, which is on schedule to begin production in early 2022. The renewable diesel leverages Global Clean Energy&#146;s patented camelina crop, which can significantly reduce life-cycle greenhouse gas emissions. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">&#147;Our expanded agreement with Global Clean Energy reinforces ExxonMobil&#146;s longstanding efforts to support society&#146;s ambitions for lower-emission
fuels,&#148; said Ian Carr, president of ExxonMobil Fuels and Lubricants Company. &#147;Through our growing relationship, we remain focused on bringing renewable fuels to market that make meaningful contributions to help consumers reduce their
emissions.&#148; </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Based on analysis of California Air Resources Board data, renewable diesel from various
<FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">non-petroleum</FONT> feedstocks can provide life-cycle greenhouse gas emissions reductions of approximately 40&nbsp;percent to 80&nbsp;percent compared to petroleum-based diesel. When cultivated as a cover crop on
rotational dryland, camelina can help meet the growing global demand for lower-carbon <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">non-petroleum</FONT> feedstocks. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">&#147;Our work at the Bakersfield biorefinery is a perfect example of what can be accomplished when an industry leader like ExxonMobil supports a growing

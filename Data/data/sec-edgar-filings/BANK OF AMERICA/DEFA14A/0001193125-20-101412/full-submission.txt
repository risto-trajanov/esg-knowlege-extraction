<P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><FONT COLOR="#ff4338"><B>Why is this year&#146;s annual meeting being held in a virtual-only format? </B></FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Our preference is to hold an <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">in-person</FONT> annual meeting of shareholders. However, due to public health concerns resulting

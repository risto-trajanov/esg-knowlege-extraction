Held on October&nbsp;2, 2020 </B></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">NEW YORK&#151; (BUSINESS WIRE) &#151; September&nbsp;10, 2020 &#151; Goldman Sachs Middle Market Lending Corp.
(&#147;MMLC&#148;) announced today a change in the location of its Special Meeting of Stockholders (the &#147;Special Meeting&#148;), at which holders of MMLC&#146;s common stock (&#147;MMLC Stockholders&#148;) will consider and vote on MMLC&#146;s
proposed merger with Goldman Sachs BDC, Inc. (&#147;GSBD&#148;), along with a related proposal for which MMLC Stockholder approval will be sought (collectively, the &#147;Proposals&#148;). In light of public health concerns relating to the COVID-19
pandemic, the Special Meeting will be held in a virtual-only format, solely by means of remote communication. As previously announced, the Special Meeting will be held on Friday, October&nbsp;2, 2020 at 10:30 a.m., Eastern Time. The virtual meeting
and live audio webcast can be accessed at: www.virtualshareholdermeeting.com/MMLC2020SM. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">As described in the proxy materials for the Special Meeting
previously distributed, MMLC Stockholders of record as of the close of business on August&nbsp;3, 2020 are entitled to receive notice of the Special Meeting and vote at the Special Meeting and any adjournments or postponements thereof. Further
information regarding the Proposals can be found in the proxy materials previously mailed to MMLC Stockholders and filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission (&#147;SEC&#148;). </P>

<FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">log-in</FONT> and test your device&#146;s browser. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>Technical Assistance</B>. A support line will be available on the

Special Meeting. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>ABOUT GOLDMAN SACHS MIDDLE MARKET LENDING CORP. </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Goldman Sachs Middle Market Lending Corp. is a specialty finance company that has elected to be regulated as a business development company under the
Investment Company Act of 1940. MMLC was formed by The Goldman Sachs Group, Inc. (&#147;Goldman Sachs&#148;) to invest primarily in middle-market companies in the United States, and is externally managed by Goldman Sachs Asset Management, L.P., <FONT
STYLE="white-space:nowrap">an&nbsp;SEC-registered&nbsp;investment</FONT> adviser and a wholly-owned subsidiary of Goldman Sachs. MMLC seeks to generate current income and, to a lesser extent, capital appreciation primarily through direct
originations of secured debt, including first lien debt, unitranche loans, including last out portions of such loans, and second lien debt, and unsecured debt, including mezzanine debt, as well as through select equity investments. </P>

</TABLE> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">You own shares of a fund managed by Goldman Sachs Asset Management (&#147;GSAM&#148; or &#147;we&#148;) and we need your
participation in this vote to help the fund reach quorum for this very important Special Meeting of Shareholders that has been adjourned to February&nbsp;19, 2021, due to the lack of shareholder participation. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B><U>What is changing? </U></B></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Specifically, this vote is
being enacted to change the Fund&#146;s <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">sub-classification</FONT> under the Investment Company Act of </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">1940 from
&#147;diversified&#148; to <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">&#147;non-diversified&#148;.</FONT> </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B><U>Why are we doing this? </U></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">As a diversified fund, the fund is generally limited as to the amount it may invest in any single issuer. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Given the weightings of the largest holdings in the fund&#146;s benchmark and the appreciation of the fund&#146;s largest holdings, we believe that it is
important to have this additional flexibility, and that the portfolio managers will be better able to execute the fund&#146;s investment strategy and other policies with this additional flexibility. For example, due to the limitations imposed on a
diversified fund&#146;s investments in any one issuer, the fund may be prevented from adding to its positions in certain stocks that the portfolio managers might view favorably. While the fund does not seek to track its benchmark, shareholders may
benefit from the flexibility afforded to <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">non-diversified</FONT> funds. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B><U>What is not changing? </U></B></P>

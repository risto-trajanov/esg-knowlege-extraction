 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:ARIAL" ALIGN="justify">You own shares of a fund managed by Goldman Sachs Asset Management (&#147;GSAM&#148; or &#147;we&#148;)
and we need your participation to obtain a sufficient number of votes at a very important Special Meeting of Shareholders that has been adjourned to <B>April</B><B></B><B>&nbsp;16, 2021</B>, due to the lack of shareholder participation. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:ARIAL" ALIGN="justify"><B><U>What is changing? </U></B></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:ARIAL" ALIGN="justify">Specifically, this
vote is being proposed to change your fund&#146;s <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">sub-classification</FONT> under the Investment Company Act of 1940 from &#147;diversified&#148; to
<FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">&#147;non-diversified&#148;.</FONT> </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:ARIAL" ALIGN="justify"><B><U>Why are we doing this? </U></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:ARIAL" ALIGN="justify">As a diversified fund, the fund is generally limited as to the amount it may invest in any single issuer. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:ARIAL" ALIGN="justify">Given the weightings of the largest holdings in the fund&#146;s benchmark and the appreciation of the fund&#146;s largest holdings, we believe that it
is important to have this additional flexibility, and that the portfolio managers will be better able to execute the fund&#146;s investment strategy and other policies with this additional flexibility. For example, due to the limitations imposed on
a diversified fund&#146;s investments in any one issuer, the fund may be prevented from adding to its positions in certain stocks that the portfolio managers might view favorably. While the fund does not seek to track its benchmark, shareholders may
benefit from the flexibility afforded to <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">non-diversified</FONT> funds. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:ARIAL" ALIGN="justify"><B><U>What is not changing? </U></B></P>

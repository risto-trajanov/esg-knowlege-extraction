 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:15pt; font-family:ARIAL"><FONT COLOR="#63666a">Fund Update Communications </FONT></P>
<P STYLE="font-size:2pt;margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt">&nbsp;</P> <P STYLE="line-height:1.0pt;margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:2pt;border-bottom:1.00pt solid #b2b2b2">&nbsp;</P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:24pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10.5pt; font-family:ARIAL" ALIGN="justify"><FONT COLOR="#63666a">Presently, proxy statements are being distributed to shareholders for the upcoming shareholder meeting scheduled for Wednesday,
October&nbsp;27, 2021. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10.5pt; font-family:ARIAL" ALIGN="justify"><FONT COLOR="#63666a">Shareholders of all funds listed in the proxy statements are being asked to elect 16
individuals (&#147;Nominees&#148;) to serve on a single, unified board with common members across the J.P. Morgan Mutual Funds and J.P. Morgan ETFs (the &#147;New Board&#148;). </FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10.5pt; font-family:ARIAL" ALIGN="justify"><FONT COLOR="#63666a">The Mutual Fund Board and the ETF Board separately determined that it is in the best interests of the J.P. Morgan Mutual Funds
and the J.P. Morgan ETFs to be overseen by the same group of trustees/directors and specifically by the individuals nominated for election. </FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10.5pt; font-family:ARIAL" ALIGN="justify"><FONT COLOR="#63666a">For additional details, please reference the Q&amp;A below. </FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10.5pt; font-family:ARIAL" ALIGN="justify"><FONT COLOR="#63666a"><B>What is the timeline for proxy materials to be sent to shareholders? </B></FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10.5pt; font-family:ARIAL" ALIGN="justify"><FONT COLOR="#63666a">The proxy materials were mailed to shareholder on or about September&nbsp;16, 2021 in preparation for a special shareholder
meeting to be held on October&nbsp;27, 2021 to address the election of the New Board. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10.5pt; font-family:ARIAL" ALIGN="justify"><FONT COLOR="#63666a"><B>Why combine the boards now?
</B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10.5pt; font-family:ARIAL" ALIGN="justify"><FONT COLOR="#63666a">From a governance perspective and at this point in the evolution of the J.P. Morgan mutual fund and ETF
businesses, it makes sense to combine the fund boards, subject to shareholder approval. JPMorgan launched its first ETF in 2015 and 6 years later they have more than 35 products, managing over $60&nbsp;billion globally across a range of asset
classes and investment styles. In light of the continued convergence of mutual funds and ETFs, we believe that shareholders will now be well-served by combining the substantial expertise of the J.P. Morgan Mutual Fund and J.P. Morgan ETF boards.
</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10.5pt; font-family:ARIAL" ALIGN="justify"><FONT COLOR="#63666a"><B>How do the J.P. Morgan Mutual Fund and J.P. Morgan ETF Boards recommend shareholders vote? </B></FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10.5pt; font-family:ARIAL" ALIGN="justify"><FONT COLOR="#63666a">After careful consideration, the current Boards of Trustees of the J.P. Morgan Mutual Funds and J.P. Morgan ETFs each unanimously
recommended that shareholders vote &#147;FOR ALL&#148; the Nominees. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10.5pt; font-family:ARIAL" ALIGN="justify"><FONT COLOR="#63666a"><B>Why are the current Trustees recommending
that that shareholders approve each of the Nominees? </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10.5pt; font-family:ARIAL" ALIGN="justify"><FONT COLOR="#63666a">In reaching its determination to recommend shareholder
approval of the Nominees, the current Mutual Fund and ETF Trustees carefully considered the experience, qualifications, attributes, and skills of each Nominee, as well as the potential benefits of moving to a unified board structure. The conclusions
reached by the Trustees were based on a comprehensive evaluation of all of the information provided and were not the result of any one factor. </FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10.5pt; font-family:ARIAL" ALIGN="justify"><FONT COLOR="#63666a"><B>When will the new Board be effective? </B></FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10.5pt; font-family:ARIAL" ALIGN="justify"><FONT COLOR="#63666a">The New Board will only take effect if approved by shareholders. If shareholders approve, the New Board is expected to take
effect in early 2022. </FONT></P>
